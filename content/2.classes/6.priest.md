### Twisted Faith
Mind Flay and Mind Blast deal 50% increased damage to targets afflicted with your Shadow Word: Pain.

::code-group
  ::code-block{label="Alliance"}

    Discoverble near Silver Stream Mine, Loch Modan:
    - You will find the entrance(35,19) to the mine.
    - Slay Kolbolds to loot **Offering Coin**(36,19)
    - In the same cave, throw the Offering Coin into a well.
    - You will receive Memory of the Devout Champion
    ***
    Discoverable near Gold Coast Quarry, Westfall:
    - You will find the entrance(32,43) to the quarry.
    - Slay Undying Laborer to loot Memory of the Devout Champion
    ***
    Discoverable in the Ruins of Mathystra, Darkshore
    - Slay Stormscale Nagas in the ruins for **Shatterspear Offering**
    - Jump into the underwater temple(59,23)
    - Locate a green statue **Shatterspear Idol** in the water.
    - Offer the **Shatterspear Offering** to the statue to recieve Memory of the Devout Champion
  ::
  ::code-block{label="Horde"}
    
    Discoverable in Silverpine Forest
    - Find the cave entrance south of Ambermill(60,73)
    - Slay Wailing Spirit for Memory of the Devout Champion
   ***
    Discoverable in the Barrens
    - Slay Razormane mobs for Helping Hand(55,27)
    - At first, the hand is closed until you help someone
    - Resurrect a player to unlock the Helping Hand
    - Convert the Helping Hand into Twisted Faith
  ::
::
